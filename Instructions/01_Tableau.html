Citi Bike Analysis - Observations

Below link will bring up a basic Tableau Dashboard that was used to curate data from the CitiBike site in order to identify patterns.

As a preliminary analysis, data for 3-day v. 24hr purchases were compared for Q1. As expected, the trend shows lesser activity during the winter months. However, it also shows a consistent upward trend as the week starts on a Monday and hitting a peak on Saturdays and Sundays. On a seasonal expectation, the trend supports the expectation that either purchase continues to trend upwards as Spring approaches. Hence, it is expected to continue trending upwards as the summer season arrives.

Citi Bike Analysis - Insights & Recommendations

The comparison of 24 hour v. 3-day purchases shows a substantial difference early in Quarterly, subsiding slightly as the season becomes more conventient for cyclists. This presents an opportunity to leverage the inventory of available Citi Bikes during the low season. The right marketing program will encourage the use of Citi Bikes early on for current customers, and bring new customers into the program. Similar to the airline industry, a simple yet very effective marketing program is to promote a 'frequent user' credit for customers. The more they purchase during the low season, the more points they will have available for the summer season.

Additional analytical studies will be applied, and the data baseline will be enriched to include multiple years.
